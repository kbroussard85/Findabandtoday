## Our Competitive Edge: "The Compliance Shield"
Most competitors act as directories. FABT acts as a **Management Engine**. By handling the flow of funds and the associated tax reporting (1099-K), we solve the #1 pain point for club owners: **Paperwork.**

## Target ICPs
1.  **The Working Artist:** Local gigging bands who need consistent work and tax compliance.
2.  **The DIY Booker:** Venue owners/managers who want to "Swipe to Book" without chasing W-9 forms.
