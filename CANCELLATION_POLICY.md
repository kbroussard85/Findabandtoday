# FABT Cancellation & Refund Policy (Draft v1.0)

## 1. The Booking Guarantee
All gig payments are held in a **Secure Escrow (via Stripe Connect)**. FABT will release funds to the Artist 24 hours after the scheduled performance time, provided no dispute has been filed by the Venue.

## 2. Venue Cancellations (Demand Side)
If a Venue cancels a confirmed booking, the following refund schedule applies to the **Gig Total**:
*   **30+ Days Prior:** 100% refund to Venue (minus FABT Service Fees).
*   **7 to 29 Days Prior:** 50% refund to Venue. The remaining 50% is paid to the Artist as a "Kill Fee."
*   **Less than 7 Days Prior:** No refund. The Artist is paid in full (100%).
*   *Note: FABT Service Fees are non-refundable to cover processing and administrative costs.*

## 3. Artist No-Shows & Cancellations (Supply Side)
This is a critical violation of platform trust.
*   **Immediate Action:** The Venue receives a **100% Refund** (including all Service Fees).
*   **Artist Penalty:**
    *   **Financial:** The Artist is charged a penalty fee equal to the FABT Service Fee to cover the platform's loss.
    *   **Reputation:** An automatic **"No-Show" flag** is added to the Artist's profile.
    *   **Termination:** Two (2) No-Shows in a 12-month period result in **Permanent Deactivation** from the platform.

## 4. Dispute Resolution ("The Performance Quality" Clause)
If a Venue believes the Artist failed to deliver the agreed-upon services (e.g., extremely late, significantly shorter set, or violation of technical rider):
*   The Venue must click **"Open Dispute"** within 24 hours of the gig end time.
*   FABT will hold the funds and act as an arbitrator. Both parties have 48 hours to provide evidence (videos, setlists, witness statements).
*   FABT's decision is final and may result in a partial or full refund.

## 5. Force Majeure
In the event of "Acts of God" (natural disasters, venue structure failure, government shutdowns):
*   The booking is cancelled without penalty to either party.
*   The Venue receives a 100% refund of the Gig Total.
*   The FABT Service Fee is converted into a **Platform Credit** for the Venue's next booking.
